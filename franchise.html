About ChittiWear Pvt. Ltd.

ChittiWear Pvt. Ltd. is a Govt. Registered, ISO certified, Trade Marked & Google Certified Company in India. We are now the India’s one of the biggest uniform manufacturer operating all over the world. Leader in Uniform Business since 2009.

ChittiWear Pvt. Ltd. Has specialized in providing school / Institutional uniforms for and public Institutions all over the world.

We carry all major institutional uniform brands, alongside our very own customized styles to meet any Institutions unique and attractive uniform needs. We offering highest quality merchandise at the most competitive prices.
